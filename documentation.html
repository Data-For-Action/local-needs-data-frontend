NPC local Needs Databank metadata tool Documentation

Here you will find documentation about the tool including the data standard. We hope this will support you in using the tool effectively to easily upload your data easily

What data are we hoping you will provide to the databank?

We have designed the databank to be flexible in the data you can upload as need can mean different things to different people. As such our standard allows you to define what your data is related to

We have based the databank on the idea of an 'observation' something you have observed that might indicate need. For example you may have observed people asking you for support via one or more of your services. If this instance you would upload the number of people you have supported, and indicate this as a 'value'

Alongside indication the value of your observation, we would ask that you define one or more properties, such as where (i.e location) this took place, or when, (i.e. the time period or date) it took place

The metadata tool allows you to add these details to your csv upload, either for the whole file or for individual columns

We also ask you to add a description of the data and who the pubisher is. In most cases the publisher will be your organisation

  • Identify areas where demand is higher or lower that the mean
  • Indicate areas where other factors, such as funding or deprivation might impact iupon demand or capacity
  • Demonstrate the breadth and depth of charitable provision to meet local need.
